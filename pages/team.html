Team


Mr. Chandrakant J. Shah

Director

         Qualified as a Bachelor of Science (Chemistry) from University of Mumbai, he made a modest beginning in mid 1950s by working as a chemist with Sterling Chemicals. He then established his own business into dye intermediates by incorporating Sterling Orgo and Inorgo Chemicals Pvt. Ltd . He later expanded his business by taking over Trichem Laboratories (Bombay) Pvt. Ltd. and setting up a chemical plant. He also promoted Chemical Corp Pvt. Ltd. with his brother Mr. Harshad Shah. At present he looks after the overall operations of the group.

Mr. Harshad J. Shah

Director

        Qualified as a Bachelor of Science (Chemistry) from University of Mumbai, he made a humble beginning by trading of chemicals. He was instrumental in setting up Marketing and Sales for Trichem Laboratories (Bombay) Pvt. Ltd. He established Chemical Corp Pvt. Ltd. and is the primary promoter. At present he manages the trading vertical and overall operations of the group.

Mr. Nilesh C. Shah

Director

He is responsible for helping set up the plant and running it, at Navi Mumbai. He solely set up the current manufacturing unit at Ambernath. He handles the day to day operations of the Ambernath unit and was instrumental in forming the core team of the company. He has a crucial role in the HR department of the entire group. Due to his technical know-how and in-depth knowledge, he has helped develop many new products for the group.
