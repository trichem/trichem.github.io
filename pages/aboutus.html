About US

History Vision

History

         Chemical Corp established itself as a partnership firm, in 1959 promoted by Mr Chandrakant Shah and Mr Harshad Shah . In 2005, it was converted into a Private Limited Company, Chemical Corp Pvt Ltd (CCPL)

         In the past 6 decades, CCPL moved from trading in Petrochemicals & Acids to becoming one of the most trusted names in supply & service amongst major users all over the country.


At Present

         We now specialize in the marketing of quality products in India that are sourced from globally acclaimed manufacturers. The products offered are prepared from high-grade components in compliance with industry guidelines. Our products are widely recognized by the customers for their high purity, accurate composition and longer shelf life.


What we provide

         Our products are widely used in the field of chemicals, polymers, petroleum products, pharmaceuticals and bulk drugs, printing inks, resins, specialty coatings, additives, adhesives, perfumery & fragrance, textile and many more industries. With its process specialization in marketing, distribution and sourcing, CCPL represents 35 global giants, which also include many of the finest Indian business houses. It provides a basket of services and end-to-end solutions to customers.
         We import from countries like Taiwan, Korea, Saudi, Japan, China, Czech, Czech, Malaysia, U.S.A, Italy, Germany, Israel, Russia(to name a few). CCPL continues to grow in a variety of market segments, responding to the fast-changing needs of its customers with unrivalled customer service and satisfaction.


Our Strengths

We provide quality products of reputed make from across the globe, with a timely techno- commercial support through a team of:

  • Knowledgeable personnel
  • Experienced and skilled manpower
  • Timely supplies of ready stock
  • Capacity and infrastructure to cater to global requirements
  • Adequate financial strength to meet the business needs
         We provide infrastructure and logistics support by way of warehousing facility of nearly 20,000 sq ft of area and have arrangements with various logistics partners to ensure fast and safe movement of goods. With a view to facilitate customer's understanding of a particular product, we furnish documents like COA, MSDS, TDS and other relevant documents on request. This updates the customer thoroughly.

Our Commitment

We endeavor to be the best company to ‘work with’ and the best company ‘to buy from’. We will do this by:

  • Surpassing customers expectations and providing prompt, efficient and better services;
  • Building and Maintaining Customer Relationships;
  • Conducting our Business economically with sound business principles.




Vision - Mission - Values




Vision

CCPL endeavors to be a recognized leader in our markets. We will realize our vision by:
  • Investing in manpower, technology, and assets.
  • Determination to preserve the environment.
  • Thrive to develop a cooperative and strong relationship with our customers.
  • Retaining customer satisfaction as our ultimate goal.

Mission

Our mission is continued growth and diversification for bottom line results with risks well contained. In order to surpass client's expectations and to maintain our market goodwill & credibility, we will:
  • Strive earnestly to meet the expectations & aspirations of our customers, in terms of quality, consistency & compatibility of our products.
  • Maintain the highest standards of integrity, value & business ethics.
  • Innovate & develop customer centric solutions to suit diverse applications.
We believe that our people are the most valuable asset for organizational and personal growth. We treat our people with dignity and look after the safety and welfare of individuals and their families.

Values

Corporate Vision and Mission derive from those that are the foundation of shared values that guide the activities of CCPL and form the basis of our way of being and working. At Chemical Corp, we know that our people are the heart of our success. We know that it’s not just about what we achieve together, but also how we achieve it. We believe in our brand and that our values are reflected in our unwavering commitment to our brand. Therefore, our main values are:

Innovation
We leverage creative market insights and expertise to maintain and grow our competitive advantage. We create breakthroughs by relentlessly asking "why" and "what if." We act decisively and accept inherent risks to bring innovative approaches and practical solutions to our markets.
Customer Focus
Our actions are driven by customer insights and helping them create value. We continuously seek ways to exceed the expectations of our customers for optimal customer satisfaction.
Honesty & Integrity
Our actions match our words and we always deliver on our promises. With a foundation of honesty and integrity as the center of our values, we strive to always do the right thing. We actively engage in discussions and commit to decisions once they are made.
Humility
We are open to personal change and continuous improvement and have a hunger to learn. We value and seek honest feedback.
Communication
We love success and achieve the same by making people feel valued and appreciated. We encourage them to be their best selves by providing coaching and feedback. We strive to implement a “can do” attitude to get the job done.
Performance Driven Team
We act as owners of the company with a bias for action and a can-do attitude. We contribute our best to the team and accept personal responsibility for our performance and development.
Sense of Purpose
Team member’s value and support each other. There's a strong focus on results and solutions, a sense of priorities, and clarity about directions and how to act. Team members express their thoughts and feelings openly, and conflict is surfaced and resolved routinely. Team members are encouraged to assume leadership role to optimize skill knowledge and personal strength.
