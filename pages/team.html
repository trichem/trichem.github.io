Team


Mr. Chandrakant J. Shah

Director

         Qualified as a Bachelor of Science from University of Mumbai, he made a modest beginning in the mid 1950s by working as a chemist with Sterling Chemicals. He, along with his brother Mr. Harshad Shah established and promoted Chemical Corp. He later diversified into manufacturing of inorganic and organic salts under the company Sterling Orgo and Inorgo Pvt. Ltd.
         He was also instrumental in setting up a manufacturing unit for Trichem Laboratories (Bombay) Pvt. Ltd. at Navi Mumbai. At present, he looks after the overall operations of the group.

Mr. Harshad J. Shah

Director

         Qualified as a Bachelor of Science (B. Sc) from University of Mumbai, he made a humble beginning by trading of chemicals. He continued to expand, his business by penetrating into the market and maintaining a strong hold on the trading vertical of the group.
         He was instrumental in establishing Chemical Corp in 1959 along with his elder brother, Mr. Chandrakant Shah. At present he manages the trading business and overall operations of the Company. He was also the Hon. Secretary of Chemical & Alkali Merchants Association (CAMA) and is presently a member of the Arbitration committee.

Mr. Nilesh C. Shah

Mr. Ketan C. Shah
